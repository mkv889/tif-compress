## 1. Brand Personality
The Firefox brand is defined by four core personality traits:
- **Opinionated**: Driven by strong convictions, grounding everything in ethos.
- **Open**: Open-minded, open-source, transparent, and global.
- **Radical**: Radically optimistic about the future of the internet.
- **Kind**: Empathetic, collaborative, and human-centric.

## 2. Typography

### Typefaces
- **Firefox Sharp Sans**: Distinctive font used for logo lockups.
- **Metropolis**: Primary font for headlines on marketing and product web experiences. (Sans Geometric, similar to Sharp Sans).
- **Inter**: Secondary typeface for paragraph text and small UI elements. Fallback for headlines if Metropolis is unavailable.

## 4. Logos & Usage
- **Parent Brand Logo**: Represents the entire ecosystem. Preferred usage is Icon + Wordmark.
- **Logotype**: Always Black or White.
- **Clear Space**: Use the uppercase 'F' (or 40% of logomark height) as a guide.
- **Backgrounds**: Full color logos only on very light or very dark backgrounds.

## 5. Visual System
- **Shape System**: Derived from product logo geometry. Used for background patterns, illustrations, and pictograms.
- **Iconography**: Pictograms and spot illustrations follow the geometric shape system.
